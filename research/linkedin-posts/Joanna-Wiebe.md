One beat a day for the next 36 days. Welcome to Day One, Beat One: 

BEAT #1: CORE PROBLEM MATCH

Every customer you have was born out of a problem.

This is the problem your prospect had before they ever heard of you. 

The thing that was already going on in their life that sent them looking for a fix.

It is NOT a problem with your product. 
It is NOT in a support ticket. 👈

The prospect doesn't care about any of that yet. 

They care about the tooth they can't bite on. 
The wasted ad spend.
The doomscrolling on LinkedIn. 
Their handbag embarrassing them at lunch with new friends.
The livestream that makes them feel amateur.

Every customer you have was born out of a problem. 

Even if that problem doesn't show up on the page (cough it should cough), you've gotta not just know it but feel it so deeply when writing for them that it could make your tooth hurt / wallet clench / thumb stop / shoulders slump / face go red.

One rule: only match a core problem your product genuinely solves. Don't poke a pain you can't fix.

HOW TO FIND IT:

Mine it, of course. VOC, baby! 

On your confirmation page, ask: 

"What was going on in your life that brought you to ________ today?"

________ = grab the weightloss kit
________ = use our AI platform
________ = book a babysitter with us

WHERE TO USE IT:

→ the ad that pulls in cold leads 
→ the top of your landing page 
→ the top of your sales page 
→ the first 30 seconds of your webinar 
→ the open of your VSL
