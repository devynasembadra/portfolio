## Original Post
💡 Are we afraid AI will take your job? No one raises their hand. 

Saw it live last week at the EMAS conference during presentation of Alex Bloemendal about AI. 
 
Obviously a big part is afraid of their job, 
but who is ready to admit it? (in a full room of peers)

Reflection: It’s not the right question. 
 
For marketers, the better question is:

Are you afraid your job will change, but you won’t change?
Because that’s the real fear.

Not that AI suddenly replaces every marketer overnight.
But that the world changes and work shifts.
 The tools change.
 The expectations change.
 The speed changes.
 The job of a marketer changes

The skills that used to make you valuable become less valuable or even evaporate.

And while some people build new ways of working and shift…
Others keep doing the same things they’ve always done.
Until one day, the market no longer needs what it needed.

That’s the discomfort.

AI probably won’t take your job in one dramatic moment. 

So maybe the question isn’t: “Will AI replace me?”
Maybe it’s: “Can I transform today into the person that brings value tomorrow”
 
 Aka: “Can you bring the Chicken”
